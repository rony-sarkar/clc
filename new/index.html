Blue Thumb Spica Support Splint 

Click on the Image to Enlarge


Who is this support for?

The Actesso Blue Neoprene Thumb Support is the ideal support to aid in the healing of the thumb and wrist from Sprains, De Quervain's Tendonitis or Arthritis. It is however recommended that you speak with your doctor if you are unsure of your medical condition or if you are sensitive to neoprene or any other material.

Sizing

This support comes in one universal size, but please select whether you require it for the left or right hand in the drop-down box at the top of this page.

How does it work?

This thumb support helps the area to heal by partially immobilising the thumb with the splint, while also applying gentle compression the the whole wrist region, helping to alleviate pain . The neoprene also helps to retain natural body heat, which increases blood flow to the area, speeding up the healing process and providing relief for conditions such as arthritis.

Features

  • Helps alleviate pain caused by a variety of thumb injuries
  • Prevents further injury
  • Supports weak muscles
  • Provides support to the wrist region
  • Neoprene material provides compression and support
  • Universal size can be adjusted to fit most people

Fitting

1. Align the splint with the outside of your thumb and loop the strap around your thumb.

2. Pull the long neoprene strap around the outside of your hand and around the wrist.

3. Attach the strap to the top of the support.
